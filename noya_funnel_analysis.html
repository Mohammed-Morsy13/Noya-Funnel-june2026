Read-out

Root causeThe drop is concentrated in paid social + Google. Snapchat lost ~1,330 sessions (−74%), Google −737 (−61%), TikTok −38%. These are your media-buying channels — the pattern points to reduced/paused ad spend or campaign delivery, not a site problem.

StrengthInstagram is now your best channel — top by sessions (969) and by far the best converter (2.37% vs ~1% for others). It also held up best (−29%). It carries half of all purchases (23 of 46).

Hidden issueSnapchat & Google buy traffic that barely converts (0.95% and 0.42%). Even before the drop they were low-efficiency. Spend lost there hurt volume but not much revenue.

What to do

  • Fix Snapchat first. It's the largest and most consistent loss (−74% to −85% across every period). Check spend, pacing and paused/disapproved campaigns for Jun 15–21.
  • Shift budget toward Instagram. It's the highest-ROI channel and already growing — scaling it recovers purchases faster than restoring low-converting Snapchat/Google traffic.
  • Re-evaluate Snapchat & Google ROI. Both convert under 1% in every period — question whether they deserve their old budget at all.
  • Hold TikTok steady. It's roughly flat week-over-week and converting at its best level of the month — no urgent action, monitor.
